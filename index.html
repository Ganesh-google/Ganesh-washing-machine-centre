About Ganesh Washing Machine Repair Centre

Fifteen Years Of Fixing Washing Machines The Right Way

Ganesh Washing Machine Repair Centre started as a two-person doorstep repair service in Kathmandu and has grown into one of the valley's most called-upon names for washing machine troubleshooting, part replacement and installation. We built our reputation the old-fashioned way: by turning up when we say we will, explaining the fault in plain language, and charging exactly what we quote.

Every technician on our team has hands-on experience across front load, top load and semi-automatic machines, and is trained to work on the electronics (PCB and control boards) as well as the mechanical side (motors, drums, bearings and pumps). That combination means most faults are diagnosed correctly on the first visit, so you are not paying for repeat call-outs.

We serve households, apartments, hostels and small businesses across Kathmandu, Lalitpur and Bhaktapur, and we keep a stock of commonly needed spare parts on hand so that straightforward repairs can often be completed in a single visit.

What We Repair

Washing Machine Services We Provide

From a machine that won't drain to one that won't switch on at all — our technicians carry a full diagnostic kit and common spare parts to resolve most issues on the spot.

🌀

Front Load Repair

Door lock faults, drum bearing noise, drainage errors and control panel issues on front-loading washing machines.

🪣

Top Load Repair

Agitator, spin cycle, timer and lid switch problems on top-loading and semi-automatic washing machines.

⚡

Motor & PCB Repair

Diagnosis and replacement of burnt motors, capacitors and faulty control boards (PCB) causing power or cycle failures.

💧

Water Leakage Fixes

Tracing and sealing leaks from door gaskets, hoses, pumps and inlet valves before they damage your flooring.

🔩

Drum & Bearing Repair

Fixing loud spinning noise, drum wobble and worn bearings that shorten the life of your machine.

🧰

Installation & Maintenance

New machine installation, plumbing connection, and scheduled maintenance visits to prevent future breakdowns.
